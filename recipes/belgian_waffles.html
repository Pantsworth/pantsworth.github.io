BELGIAN WAFFLES

Michael's Notes:
Getting a good egg white whip is crucial. Can be done by hand or with a mixer. Take your pick.

INGREDIENTS


1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 eggs separated
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
YIELD:Waffles. Plan to make a double batch and freeze some.
ESTIMATED TIME: 30-45 min

DIRECTIONS


0. Preheat waffle maker, spray or brush on vegetable oil

1. Sift baking powder, flour and salt in bowl

2. Beat egg whites to stiff peaks until foamy, sprinkle in sugar, then beat to stiff peaks

3. Blend egg yolks, milk, oil together

4. Add dry ingredients to egg yolk mixture

5. Fold egg whites into batter
