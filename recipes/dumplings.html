Dumplings

Originally from Grandma Joy.

INGREDIENTS


2 cups sifted flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. shortening
3/4 cup milk

Broth:
2 quarts broth (season with salt & pepper)
1 cup milk

YIELD: DUMPLINGS FOR 4


DIRECTIONS


1. Place broth and milk into saucepan and bring to a slow rolling boil.

2. While the broth is heating, sift dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, and salt) together, cut in shortening. Add milk to make a firm dough - roll very thin - cut in strips 1 in by 4 in.

2 (Large Batches). If making a large batch, Mix flour with salt and baking powder in food processor until blended; add shortening and mix until looks like cornmeal; add milk and blend just until loosely mixed.Remove from processor and roll out small portions of the dough very thin. Cut into strips about 1"x 4".

3. Drop dumplings into the boiling broth one at a time, moving dumplings with a fork so they will not stick together.

4. "Good luck" - Joy Arconati
