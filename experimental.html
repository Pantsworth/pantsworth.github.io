SLITSCANNR

Created a functional slit-scanning camera using Magic Lantern, a Canon 6D, and a Python backend. The images rendered maintain high-quality image data, and the process is easy enough for almost anyone to pursue. Might one day become a web/standalone app.


Research project for EECS 395: Computational Photography Seminar at Northwestern University.

More on slitscans:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slit-scan_photography
